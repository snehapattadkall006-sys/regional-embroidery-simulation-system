About RESS

Bridging centuries of artistry with modern digital technology

Our Mission

The Regional Embroidery Simulation System was born out of a deep reverence for India's extraordinary textile heritage. With thousands of embroidery traditions at risk of fading, we created this platform to document, celebrate, and digitally preserve these precious art forms.

We believe technology and tradition need not be at odds — digital tools can breathe new life into ancient arts, making them accessible to new generations of artists, designers, and culture enthusiasts worldwide.
