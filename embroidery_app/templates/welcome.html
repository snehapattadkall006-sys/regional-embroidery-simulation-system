About Us

Preserving India's Embroidery Heritage Digitally

India is home to some of the world's most exquisite embroidery traditions — each stitch a thread connecting us to centuries of culture, community, and artistry.

The Regional Embroidery Simulation System (RESS) was created to preserve, document, and digitally simulate these precious art forms for future generations. From the delicate white-on-white Chikankari of Lucknow to the vibrant mirror work of Kutch — each style carries the soul of its region.

Our Mission

Our Objectives

01

Preserve Embroidery Heritage

Document and digitize India's endangered embroidery traditions before they fade from collective memory, ensuring survival for future generations.

02

Digital Visualization

Enable users to visualize how different embroidery patterns look on actual fabrics through our advanced simulation technology.

03

Cultural Awareness

Bridge the gap between traditional artisan communities and modern audiences by sharing authentic cultural knowledge and stories.

04

Educational Learning

Provide comprehensive learning resources including techniques, tutorials, and historical context to promote appreciation of textile arts.
